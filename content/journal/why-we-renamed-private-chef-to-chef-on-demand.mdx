<p>For years, we called it "Private Chef." It was accurate — Chef Ameet would come to your home, cook an incredible multi-course Indian Fusion dinner, and leave your kitchen spotless. But something about the name never quite captured what the experience actually felt like.</p>

<p>"Private Chef" sounds like a luxury reserved for the ultra-wealthy. Something you'd see in a magazine about billionaires. That's not Elephant Wings.</p>

<h2>What Chef-On-Demand Actually Means</h2>

<p>Chef-On-Demand Kansas City is about accessibility. It's about a couple celebrating their anniversary with a 5-course Spice Route Journey in their own dining room. It's about a group of friends who want something more memorable than a restaurant reservation. It's about you — hosting an evening where the only requirement is your presence.</p>

<p>We handle everything else. The shopping. The prep. The cooking. The plating. The cleanup. You just show up to your own table.</p>

<h2>The Menu Experience</h2>

<p>Our Chef-On-Demand menus are built around real ingredients and real technique. Turmeric-infused bites. Hand-crafted samosas. Braised lamb in a slow-cooked masala reduction. Cardamom-scented Gulab Jamun with rose cream.</p>

<p>Every menu is customizable for Vegan, Gluten-Free, and Halal dietary needs. Because bold flavor shouldn't have to come with compromises.</p>
